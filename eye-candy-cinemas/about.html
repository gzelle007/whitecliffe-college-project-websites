of going to the movies, smell of popcorn in the air and the emotions that emanate from an
amazing performance coupled with breath-taking graphics.

We are want to bring these experiences to you!

Covid-19 Health and Safety Protocols

We clean and disinfect all objects and surfaces after every show. To help
contain the virus, please help us protecting yourself and others:

  • Stay home if you're sick
  • Scan or sign in at the contact tracing register
  • Turn on your Bluetooth tracing
  • Wear a face covering
  • Cough or sneeze into your elbow
  • Wash and sanitize your hands
  • Place tissues in the bins provided
For updates and more information on keeping yourself safe, visit www.Covid19.govt.nz.
