Who We Are

THE RIGHT BUILDERS FOR YOU

McG Builders is a family owned and operated company based out of central Auckland. We are entire build specialists, from an empty plot to a full scale renovation, our team is qualified, friendly and up for the challenge, big or small. We hold ourselves to the highest quality of workmanship in delivering you your dream home.

Providing building process consultations from start to finish. Our services include project management, council applications, choosing the right architect or interior designer to match your taste. Delivering your dream home on time on budget, we pride ourselves on the our eye for detail resulting in high quality finish.

Our director Cody Mackay has 16 years experience in the building industry working as a residential carpenter in NZ, Australia and UK. Cody began building in 2005 and became a qualified builder in 2010. He has been working in the commercial sector as a supervisor and manager since 2016. Cody has worked with an extensive range of materials on top end homes in London, new builds in Auckland, top level renovations, new commercial apartment buildings, weather-tightness remediation and refurbishments.

Certified in site safe and first aid trained, we take precautions to ensure the job is done safely and with the upmost of care.
